# Code of Conduct

This project follows the
[Contributor Covenant](https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct/),
v2.1. The short version:

- Be respectful, constructive, and specific.
- Disagree with ideas, not people.
- No harassment of any kind in issues, PRs, discussions, or any other
  project space.

Report violations privately to `conduct@attentionlabs.ai`. Maintainers will
respond within five business days. Decisions are final at the maintainers'
discretion.
